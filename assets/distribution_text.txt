<h1>Welcome!</h1>
<p>Create Missiles is a light-hearted missile mod that adds realistically physically simulated missiles to Create. Has JEI and Ponder integration to help you get started.</p>
<p>Currently available for&nbsp;<strong>1.20.1</strong> <strong>Forge</strong> and <strong>Fabric</strong>, and requires both <strong>Create 5.0.1</strong> and <strong>Architectury API</strong>.</p>
<p><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_653313ca-7ae1-42ac-8ae9-a8fb81ab8ec2.png" width="100%"></p>
<h1>Features</h1>
<p>Here's a list of some of the features in the mod if you don't know where to start</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Clone assemblies with paper</strong>&nbsp;<span class="spoiler">(writing this first because there's currently no JEI for this recipe)</span></li>
<li>Launch a <del>phantom</del> 'Drone' that flies to a specific coordinate and makes a map of the area</li>
<li>Assemble a Launch Pad multi-block structure with a Control Panel, Assembly Panel and a Navigation Panel</li>
<li>Missiles and drones can travel across unloaded chunks</li>
<li>Explosions are handled asynchronously&nbsp;and utilise multiple threads, meaning we can afford to have more complex destruction calulations including the ability for blast-resistant blocks to protect other blocks</li>
<li>Customize your missiles by combining different Warheads, Chassis and Thrusters</li>
<li>Find upgrade cores in Bunkers across the world that make missiles more powerfull</li>
<li>Plot a target in the Navigation Panel by clicking on a map and setting the burn duration</li>
<li>Unique warheads like the Biome Brush that modifies biomes</li>
</ul>
